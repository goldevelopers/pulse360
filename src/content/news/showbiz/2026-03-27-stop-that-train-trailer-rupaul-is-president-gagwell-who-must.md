## New Action-Comedy "Stop That Train!" Features RuPaul in a Leading Role

Bleecker Street and World of Wonder have unveiled the first trailer for their upcoming action-comedy film, "Stop That Train!" Directed by Adam Shankman, the film showcases an intriguing blend of humor and adventure, with a notable performance from RuPaul, who takes on the role of President Gagwell.

### Plot Overview

In "Stop That Train!", RuPaul's character, President Gagwell, finds himself in a race against time to prevent a disastrous train wreck. The film promises to deliver not only comedic moments but also thrilling sequences as Gagwell navigates the challenges that come with his high-stakes mission. The premise offers a unique twist on the action-comedy genre, combining elements of political satire with the urgency of a disaster scenario.

### Star-Studded Cast

The film features a diverse cast, including alumni from the popular reality series "RuPaul's Drag Race." Best friends Tess, played by Ginger Minj, and DeeDee, portrayed by Jujubee, serve as train stewardesses who bring their own flair and humor to the storyline. Their characters are expected to add depth and levity to the film, enhancing the dynamic between the protagonists as they work alongside President Gagwell to avert the impending crisis.

### Creative Team

Adam Shankman, known for his work in both film and television, directs "Stop That Train!" His previous credits include a variety of successful projects that span different genres, indicating a well-rounded approach to storytelling. The collaboration between Bleecker Street and World of Wonder suggests a strong commitment to producing a film that resonates with audiences, particularly those familiar with the drag and LGBTQ+ communities.

### Anticipation and Reception

The release of the trailer has generated excitement among fans of RuPaul and the broader entertainment community. As the film blends elements of comedy, action, and social commentary, it is poised to attract a wide audience. The inclusion of drag culture in mainstream cinema continues to gain traction, and "Stop That Train!" may further contribute to this trend.

### Conclusion

"Stop That Train!" is set to be a noteworthy addition to the action-comedy landscape, with its unique premise and a cast that brings both talent and charisma to the screen. As the film approaches its release date, anticipation builds for how it will be received by audiences and critics alike. With RuPaul leading the charge, the film is expected to deliver a mix of laughter, excitement, and perhaps a few poignant moments, making it a must-watch for fans of the genre.
