## Azzi Fudd Shines in Final Home Game as UConn Advances to Sweet 16

In a commanding display of basketball, Azzi Fudd led the University of Connecticut (UConn) to a resounding 98-45 victory over Syracuse on Monday night. The win not only marked a significant milestone for the team but also served as a fitting farewell for Fudd in her final game at Gampel Pavilion.

### Fudd's Impressive Performance

Fudd, a standout player for UConn, showcased her exceptional skills by scoring 34 points, with an impressive 26 of those coming in the first half. Her early dominance set the tone for the game, allowing UConn to establish a commanding lead. Fudd's performance was not just a display of individual talent; it was a testament to her preparation and focus, as she credited her success to the advice she received from her teammate Geno, particularly regarding her shooting technique.

### A Historic Win

The victory over Syracuse was not only significant for Fudd but also for the UConn program, as it marked the team's 32nd consecutive appearance in the Sweet 16 of the NCAA tournament. This achievement underscores UConn's long-standing dominance in women's college basketball and their ability to consistently perform at a high level. The 31-0 run during the game exemplified the team's defensive prowess and offensive efficiency, leaving Syracuse struggling to keep pace.

### Looking Ahead

As UConn moves forward in the tournament, the team will aim to build on this momentum. The Sweet 16 matchup will be crucial as they seek to extend their championship aspirations. Fudd's leadership and scoring ability will undoubtedly play a pivotal role in UConn's quest for success in the coming rounds.

Azzi Fudd's farewell performance at Gampel Pavilion will be remembered not only for the scoreline but also for the impact she has had on the UConn program.
