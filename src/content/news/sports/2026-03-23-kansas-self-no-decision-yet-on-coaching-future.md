## Kansas Coach Bill Self Reflects on Future After Season-Ending Loss

In the aftermath of a disappointing season-ending loss to St. John's, Kansas Jayhawks head coach Bill Self addressed the media regarding his future with the program. The veteran coach, who has been at the helm of the Jayhawks for nearly two decades, stated that he has not yet made a decision about whether he will continue coaching.

Following the game, which marked the conclusion of a challenging season for Kansas, Self emphasized that his forthcoming decision will be primarily influenced by his health. "I have not made a decision yet," Self remarked during the post-game press conference. "Any choice I make will be based solely on my health and well-being."

Self's health has been a topic of concern in recent years. In 2022, he underwent a medical procedure that kept him sidelined for part of the season. His commitment to the program and the well-being of his players have always been paramount, and he reiterated that these factors will guide his next steps.

The Jayhawks concluded the season with a record that fell short of expectations, leading to questions about the team's performance and future direction. Self, who has led Kansas to multiple Big 12 championships and a national title in 2008, acknowledged the disappointment felt by fans and players alike. "We had high hopes for this season, and it’s tough to see it end this way," he said.

As one of college basketball's most respected figures, Self's potential departure would have significant implications for the Kansas program. His track record of success, including numerous NCAA Tournament appearances and player development, has solidified his legacy within the sport. However, the focus now shifts to his health and personal considerations as he weighs his options.

The Kansas athletic department and fans alike await Self's decision with bated breath, understanding the impact it could have on the future of the program. While the immediate focus remains on the aftermath of the season, the uncertainty surrounding Self's future adds another layer of complexity to the challenges facing the Jayhawks.

As the offseason approaches, Kansas will need to evaluate its roster, coaching staff, and overall strategy moving forward. The potential for change looms large, but for now, the primary concern is the well-being of one of college basketball's most enduring coaches.

In the coming weeks, Self is expected to take time to reflect on his situation, prioritizing his health and personal circumstances before making any announcements. The Kansas basketball community remains hopeful for a resolution that aligns with Self's well-being and the program's future aspirations.
