## Rare Triple Qualification in Men's 60m Semi-Finals at World Athletics Indoor Championships

In an unprecedented turn of events during the men's 60m semi-finals at the World Athletics Indoor Championships held in Poland, Belgium's Simon Verherstraeten and the Netherlands' Taymir Burnet both clocked in at an exact time of 6.56 seconds.
