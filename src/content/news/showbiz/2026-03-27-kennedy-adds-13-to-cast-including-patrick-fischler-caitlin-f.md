## Netflix Expands Cast for Upcoming Drama Series 'Kennedy'

Netflix has announced an expansion of the cast for its highly anticipated drama series, "Kennedy," which delves into the complex lives, relationships, and tragedies of the iconic Kennedy family. The streaming platform has added 13 new actors to the ensemble, including notable names such as Patrick Fischler, Caitlin Fitzgerald, Denis O’Hare, and Eddie Marsan.

### A Deep Dive into the Kennedy Legacy

"Kennedy" aims to provide an in-depth exploration of one of America's most storied families, capturing the multifaceted dynamics that defined their public and private lives. The series is expected to highlight not only the political achievements of the Kennedys but also the personal struggles and controversies that have surrounded them over the decades. 

### New Additions to the Cast

The recent casting announcement brings a wealth of talent to the project, with each actor set to portray key figures in the Kennedy saga. Patrick Fischler, known for his roles in "Mad Men" and "Once Upon a Time," will join the cast alongside Caitlin Fitzgerald, who has made a name for herself in series like "Masters of Sex." Denis O’Hare, an Emmy Award-winning actor recognized for his work in "American Horror Story," and Eddie Marsan, known for his performances in "Ray Donovan" and "The World's End," are also set to bring their considerable talents to the series.

The full list of new cast members has yet to be disclosed, but the inclusion of these seasoned actors is expected to enhance the narrative depth and emotional resonance of the series.

Alongside the casting news, Netflix has released a first-look photo from the series, providing a glimpse into the visual style and tone of "Kennedy." The image hints at a production that is both period-appropriate and visually striking, likely reflecting the historical significance of the Kennedy family while appealing to contemporary audiences.

### Anticipation and Expectations
