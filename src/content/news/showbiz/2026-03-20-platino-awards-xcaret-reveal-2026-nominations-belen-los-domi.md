## Platino Awards Xcaret Reveal 2026 Nominations: ‘Belén’ and ‘Los Domingos’ Lead the Pack

The anticipation surrounding the 13th annual Platino Awards has reached a new height with the announcement of this year’s nominations. The prestigious ceremony, which celebrates excellence in Ibero-American film and television, is set to take place on May 9, 2026, at the Gran Tlachco Theater in Xcaret Park, located in the picturesque Riviera Maya, Mexico.

### Leading Nominees

This year, two films have emerged as frontrunners in the nominations: *Belén* and *Los Domingos*, each receiving an impressive total of 11 nominations. These films have captivated audiences and critics alike, showcasing the rich storytelling and artistic talent prevalent in Ibero-American cinema.

*Belén*, a poignant narrative that explores themes of family and identity, has garnered acclaim for its powerful performances and evocative direction. Meanwhile, *Los Domingos* has been recognized for its innovative storytelling and engaging character development, making both films standout contenders in various categories.

The Platino Awards honor a wide array of categories, including Best Film, Best Director, Best Actor, and Best Actress, among others. The nominations reflect the diversity and creativity of the Ibero-American film industry, with a focus on both emerging talents and established figures.

In addition to the competitive categories, the awards also recognize significant contributions to the industry through special honors. This year’s ceremony promises to celebrate not only the nominated films but also the broader impact of Ibero-American cinema on the global stage.
