## Maggie Baird Launches 'Climate Kitchen' in Collaboration with WETA

Maggie Baird, an environmental advocate and founder of Support+Feed, is set to host a new sustainable lifestyle series titled "Climate Kitchen." The show will be produced in collaboration with WETA, a prominent public media organization based in the United States. This initiative aims to educate viewers on sustainable living practices while featuring a diverse array of guests, including Baird's children, renowned artists Billie Eilish and Finneas, as well as culinary icon Martha Stewart and climate activist Xiye Bastida.

### A Focus on Sustainability

"Climate Kitchen" is designed to explore the intersections of food, sustainability, and environmental consciousness. The series intends to provide practical solutions and insights that viewers can incorporate into their daily lives. Baird's commitment to sustainability is evident in her work with Support+Feed, which focuses on addressing food insecurity while promoting plant-based eating as a means to combat climate change.

In a statement, Baird expressed her enthusiasm for the project, stating, "I am thrilled to partner with WETA to bring 'Climate Kitchen' to life. This series is not just about cooking; it's about creating a community around sustainable living and inspiring change."

### A Star-Studded Lineup

The show will feature a variety of guests who are influential in their respective fields. Billie Eilish and Finneas, both Grammy-winning musicians, will join their mother in discussions about the impact of food choices on the environment. Martha Stewart, known for her expertise in cooking and home living, will share her own sustainable practices and recipes.

Xiye Bastida, a prominent youth climate activist, will also appear on the show, bringing her perspective on the urgent need for climate action and the role of food systems in this global challenge. The combination of these guests promises to create engaging and informative content that resonates with a wide audience.

### Educational and Entertaining

"Climate Kitchen" aims to balance education with entertainment, making sustainability accessible and enjoyable for viewers. Each episode will feature cooking segments, discussions on sustainable practices, and tips on how to reduce one's carbon footprint through food choices. The show will also highlight local and seasonal ingredients, encouraging viewers to support their communities while making environmentally conscious decisions.

### The Role of Public Media

WETA, known for its commitment to producing high-quality educational content, will provide a platform for "Climate Kitchen" to reach a broad audience. The collaboration underscores the importance of public media in addressing pressing social issues, such as climate change and food security. By leveraging its resources and expertise, WETA aims to amplify Baird's message and inspire viewers to take actionable steps toward a more sustainable lifestyle.

### Conclusion

As the world grapples with the challenges of climate change, initiatives like "Climate Kitchen" play a crucial role in fostering awareness and encouraging sustainable practices. With Maggie Baird at the helm and a lineup of influential guests, the series is poised to make a significant impact in promoting a healthier planet through informed food choices. Viewers can look forward to the premiere of "Climate Kitchen," where they will not only learn about sustainability but also be inspired to take part in the movement for a better future.
