## ‘Shadow Transit’ FilMart Success Paves Way for Act3 and BlackOps Studios Asia Co-Production ‘A Thread of Steel’

In a significant development for the Asian film industry, Hong Kong-based Act3 and Manila-based BlackOps Studios Asia have announced their first major co-production titled “A Thread of Steel.” This historical action drama marks the beginning of a formalized partnership between the two companies, following their successful collaboration on the project “Shadow Transit” showcased at the Hong Kong FilMart.

### A New Chapter in Co-Productions

The announcement of “A Thread of Steel” comes as a promising venture for both Act3 and BlackOps Studios Asia, reflecting a growing trend of cross-border collaborations in the entertainment sector. The partnership aims to leverage the strengths of both companies, combining Act3’s established presence in Hong Kong’s film landscape with BlackOps Studios Asia’s innovative storytelling and production capabilities in the Philippines.

### Key Creative Team

The film is set to star Qymira, a rising talent in the industry, who is expected to bring a compelling presence to the screen. The project will be directed by Pedring Lopez, known for his work in the action genre, which aligns well with the film's thematic elements. Lopez's experience and vision are anticipated to elevate the narrative, drawing audiences into the historical context that “A Thread of Steel” aims to explore.
