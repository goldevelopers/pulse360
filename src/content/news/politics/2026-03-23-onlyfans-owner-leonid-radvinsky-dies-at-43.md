Under Radvinsky's leadership, the platform experienced exponential growth, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic when many creators turned to digital avenues for income. The site has been credited with empowering creators, offering them a direct way to connect with their audiences and retain a larger share of their earnings compared to traditional media outlets.
