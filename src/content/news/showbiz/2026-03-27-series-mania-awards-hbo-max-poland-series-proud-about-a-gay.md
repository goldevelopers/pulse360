## HBO Max's 'Proud' Wins Big at Series Mania Awards

In a notable achievement for HBO Max, the Polish series "Proud" has garnered significant recognition at the Series Mania Awards, securing both the Grand Prize in the international competition and the Best Actor award. This dual victory highlights the series' impact and resonance within the global television landscape.

## Overview of 'Proud'

"Proud" tells the poignant story of a gay man who finds himself in a challenging situation after a family tragedy. He takes on the responsibility of caring for his infant niece, navigating the complexities of grief, love, and familial bonds. The series has been praised for its sensitive portrayal of LGBTQ+ themes and the emotional depth of its characters.

The narrative not only explores the challenges faced by the protagonist but also delves into broader societal issues surrounding acceptance and the dynamics of non-traditional family structures. This focus on personal and societal themes has contributed to the show's appeal, making it a standout in the international television scene.

## Recognition at Series Mania

The Series Mania Awards, held annually in Lille, France, celebrate excellence in television storytelling from around the world. The awards are known for showcasing innovative and impactful series, making them a prestigious platform for creators and actors alike. 

"Proud's" success at the awards is particularly significant, as it reflects a growing recognition of Polish cinema and television on the global stage. The Grand Prize is awarded to the most outstanding series, while the Best Actor accolade underscores the performance of the lead actor, who has been commended for bringing depth and authenticity to the role.
