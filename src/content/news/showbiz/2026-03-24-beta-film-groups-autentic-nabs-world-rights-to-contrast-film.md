## Autentic Acquires Global Rights to Swiss Docuseries on Credit Suisse's Downfall

In a significant development in the world of documentary filmmaking, Munich-based production company Autentic has secured the international rights to the acclaimed Swiss docuseries "Game Over – The Fall of Credit Suisse." This four-part series, directed by Simon Helbling, chronicles the dramatic collapse of the once-mighty Swiss bank in 2023 and its subsequent takeover by rival UBS.

### A Deep Dive into a Financial Crisis

"Game Over – The Fall of Credit Suisse" provides a comprehensive examination of the events leading to the bank's downfall, offering insights into the factors that contributed to its decline. Co-penned by journalist Arthur Rutishauser, the series promises to unravel the complexities of the financial landscape that surrounded Credit Suisse during its turbulent final days. 

The docuseries is not just a recounting of events; it aims to delve into the broader implications of the bank's collapse on the global financial system. By combining expert interviews, archival footage, and in-depth analysis, the series seeks to present a multifaceted view of a crisis that has resonated far beyond Switzerland's borders.

### The Significance of the Acquisition

Autentic's acquisition of the docuseries underscores the growing interest in financial documentaries, particularly those that address significant events in recent history. The collapse of Credit Suisse is a case study in corporate mismanagement, regulatory failures, and the impact of global economic trends, making it a relevant topic for audiences worldwide.

The series has already garnered attention for its meticulous storytelling and insightful commentary, positioning it as a crucial addition to the documentary genre. With Autentic's global distribution capabilities, the series is expected to reach a wide audience, further igniting discussions about the future of banking and financial regulation.

### A Closer Look at Credit Suisse's Downfall

Credit Suisse, once one of the world's leading financial institutions, faced a series of scandals and operational challenges that ultimately led to its downfall. The bank's failure to adapt to changing market conditions and its involvement in various controversies raised questions about its governance and risk management practices. The takeover by UBS, another Swiss banking giant, marked a significant moment in the financial sector, prompting debates about consolidation and the stability of the banking system.

As the series unfolds, viewers can expect to gain valuable insights into the lessons learned from Credit Suisse's collapse and the potential ramifications for the banking industry as a whole.

### Conclusion

With "Game Over – The Fall of Credit Suisse," Autentic is poised to deliver a compelling narrative that not only recounts a pivotal moment in financial history but also serves as a cautionary tale for the future. As the series prepares for its international release, it stands to contribute meaningfully to the ongoing discourse surrounding financial accountability and the evolution of banking practices in an increasingly complex global economy.
