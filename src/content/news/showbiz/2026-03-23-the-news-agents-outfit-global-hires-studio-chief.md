## Global Appoints Craig Hunter as Studio Chief for Global Studios

In a strategic move to bolster its production capabilities, Global, the creator of the popular podcast "The News Agents," has appointed Craig Hunter as the Managing Director of its newly established Global Studios. This significant appointment comes as part of a broader initiative to expand the company's footprint in the media landscape.

### Background of the Appointment

Craig Hunter, who previously served as a creative director at STV Studios, has been chosen for this pivotal role by Simon Pitts, the former STV chief who now leads Global. Hunter's extensive experience in the television industry, including a notable tenure as a commissioner at the BBC, positions him well to drive the vision and operations of Global Studios.

### The Vision for Global Studios

Global Studios was unveiled last year as part of Global's ambitious plans to diversify its content offerings and enhance its production capabilities. The studio aims to create a wide array of programming, ranging from scripted series to documentaries, catering to both traditional broadcasting and digital platforms. With Hunter at the helm, the studio is expected to leverage his creative expertise and industry connections to develop compelling content that resonates with audiences.

### Industry Reactions

The appointment of Hunter has been met with optimism within the industry. Colleagues and industry insiders have lauded his track record of successful projects and his ability to foster creative talent. Simon Pitts expressed confidence in Hunter's leadership, stating that his vision aligns with Global's goals of innovation and quality in content production.

### The Future of Global Studios

As Global Studios embarks on its journey under Hunter's leadership, the media landscape continues to evolve rapidly. The demand for diverse and high-quality content is at an all-time high, driven by the proliferation of streaming services and changing viewer habits. Global's investment in a dedicated studio reflects its commitment to staying competitive in this dynamic environment.

Hunter's immediate priorities will likely include assembling a talented team, identifying key projects, and establishing partnerships with other content creators and distributors. His background in commissioning and production will be invaluable as Global Studios seeks to carve out a niche in a crowded market.

### Conclusion

The appointment of Craig Hunter as Managing Director of Global Studios marks a significant step for Global as it aims to expand its production capabilities and enhance its content offerings. With a seasoned industry professional at the helm, Global Studios is poised to make a meaningful impact in the media landscape, creating engaging content that meets the evolving demands of audiences worldwide. As the studio begins to take shape, all eyes will be on Hunter and his team to deliver on the ambitious vision set forth by Global.
