## A Former NFL GM's Must-Draft Board for 2026: Insights from Mike Tannenbaum

As the 2026 NFL Draft approaches, former NFL General Manager Mike Tannenbaum has shared his insights on the prospects he believes teams should prioritize. Tannenbaum, who has a wealth of experience in player evaluation and team-building, has compiled a list of 14 players he deems essential targets across all seven rounds of the upcoming draft.

### A Comprehensive Approach to Drafting

Tannenbaum's draft board reflects a strategic approach that considers both immediate team needs and long-term potential. His selections range from early-round talents to hidden gems that could emerge later in the draft. This comprehensive perspective is crucial in a league where the right draft choices can significantly impact a franchise's trajectory.

### Key Prospects Highlighted

Among the prospects Tannenbaum has identified, Dillon Thieneman stands out as a player to watch on Day 1. Known for his versatility and high football IQ, Thieneman has garnered attention from scouts and analysts alike. His ability to adapt to various positions makes him a valuable asset for any team looking to enhance its roster.

As the draft progresses, Tannenbaum also emphasizes the importance of late-round selections. Jaffer Murphy, who is projected to be available on Day 3, represents the type of player who can provide significant value. While not as widely recognized as some of his counterparts, Murphy's skill set and potential for development could make him a steal for teams willing to invest in his growth.

### The Importance of a Strategic Draft Board

Creating a must-draft board is not merely about identifying the most talented players; it also involves understanding team dynamics, positional needs, and the overall draft landscape. Tannenbaum's experience as a GM allows him to navigate these complexities effectively. He emphasizes that teams should prioritize players who fit their system and culture, rather than simply selecting the highest-rated prospects.

### Looking Ahead

As teams prepare for the 2026 NFL Draft, Tannenbaum's insights serve as a valuable resource for decision-makers. His emphasis on a balanced approach—targeting both high-profile talents and under-the-radar prospects—can help franchises build more competitive rosters.

The draft is an unpredictable event, and while Tannenbaum's recommendations provide a solid foundation, the final outcomes will ultimately depend on how teams execute their strategies. As the countdown to the draft continues, all eyes will be on the prospects Tannenbaum has highlighted, as well as the decisions made by NFL teams that could shape the future of the league.

In conclusion, Mike Tannenbaum's must-draft board for 2026 offers a thoughtful perspective on the upcoming draft class. By focusing on a diverse range of players and emphasizing strategic fit, Tannenbaum provides a roadmap for NFL teams aiming to enhance their rosters through the draft.
