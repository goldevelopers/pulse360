## Bowen Yang, Matt Rogers, Joey Fatone & JC Chasez Join ‘Titanique’ Producing Team for Limited Broadway Run

The beloved musical parody “Titanique” is set to make waves on Broadway with a newly announced producing team that includes notable figures from the entertainment industry. This week, Tony-winning producer Eva Price revealed that Bowen Yang, Matt Rogers, Joey Fatone, and JC Chasez have joined the production team for the limited run of the show.

### A Unique Collaboration

“Titanique,” which cleverly intertwines the iconic film “Titanic” with the music of Celine Dion, has garnered attention for its humorous take on the classic story. The inclusion of Yang and Rogers, co-hosts of the popular podcast “Las Culturistas,” adds a fresh dynamic to the production. Bowen Yang, who gained fame for his memorable portrayal of the iceberg during “Weekend Update” on “Saturday Night Live,” brings a unique comedic perspective to the project. Matt Rogers, known for his sharp wit and engaging presence, is expected to contribute to the show's vibrant energy.

### NSYNC Stars Join the Production

The addition of Joey Fatone and JC Chasez, both members of the iconic boy band NSYNC, further enhances the musical credentials of “Titanique.” Their involvement not only reflects the show's commitment to high-quality entertainment but also taps into the nostalgia associated with their music. Fatone and Chasez's experience in the entertainment industry, coupled with their understanding of musical theater, positions them as valuable assets to the production team.

### The Vision of Eva Price

Eva Price, the Tony-winning producer behind the project, expressed her excitement about the new team members. “Bringing these incredible talents together is a dream come true,” she stated. “Their unique backgrounds and experiences will undoubtedly elevate ‘Titanique’ to new heights.” Price's vision for the show aligns with her track record of producing successful productions, making her a pivotal figure in the Broadway landscape.

### Limited Broadway Run

“Titanique” is set for a limited run on Broadway, which has sparked interest among theatergoers and fans of the original film alike. The musical's blend of comedy, music, and nostalgia promises to attract a diverse audience, making it a must-see event this season. The production aims to provide a fresh perspective on the familiar tale, ensuring that both new viewers and long-time fans find something to enjoy.

### Conclusion

As “Titanique” prepares for its Broadway debut, the addition of Bowen Yang, Matt Rogers, Joey Fatone, and JC Chasez to the producing team signals an exciting chapter for the musical. Their collective expertise and creative energy are expected to resonate with audiences, making this limited run a significant event in the theater community. With the production's unique premise and a talented team at the helm, “Titanique” is poised to make a splash on Broadway.
