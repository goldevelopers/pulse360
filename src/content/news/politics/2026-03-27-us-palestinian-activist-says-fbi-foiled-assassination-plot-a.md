## FBI Foils Alleged Assassination Plot Against Palestinian Activist Nerdeen Kiswani

In a significant development, U.S. law enforcement officials have announced the arrest of a man accused of plotting a Molotov cocktail attack against Nerdeen Kiswani, a prominent Palestinian activist based in the United States. The incident has raised concerns regarding the safety of activists and the potential for politically motivated violence.

### Arrest and Charges

The suspect, whose identity has not been disclosed, was apprehended following an investigation by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI). Authorities allege that the individual was planning to carry out an attack against Kiswani, who has been vocal about Palestinian rights and has participated in various protests and demonstrations advocating for the Palestinian cause.

According to law enforcement sources, the suspect was allegedly in possession of materials intended for the construction of Molotov cocktails, which are incendiary devices that can cause significant harm and destruction. The FBI's intervention was prompted by credible intelligence that indicated a credible threat to Kiswani's safety.
