## ‘Marshals’ Star Mo Brings Plenty Seeks Justice for Nephew: “He Was Our Future”

In a heartfelt conversation with The Hollywood Reporter, Mo Brings Plenty, known for his role in the CBS spinoff of the popular series 'Yellowstone,' has opened up about a personal tragedy that has deeply affected him and his family. The actor is advocating for justice following the untimely death of his nephew, Cole Brings Plenty, who passed away at the young age of 27 in 2024.

### A Personal Loss

Mo Brings Plenty, who has gained recognition for his compelling performances both on and off-screen, described Cole as "our future." The loss of his nephew has not only left a void in his family but has also motivated him to shine a light on the issues facing Native American communities, particularly those living on reservations. Cole Brings Plenty was also an actor, known for his role in the series '1923,' which further underscores the deep connection between their family and the entertainment industry.

### Advocacy for Reservation Stories

In his interview, Brings Plenty emphasized the importance of telling authentic stories from Native American perspectives. He believes that the narratives surrounding reservation life are often overlooked in mainstream media, and he is determined to change that. "We need to share our stories, our struggles, and our triumphs," he stated, highlighting the necessity of representation in Hollywood.

The actor's commitment to advocacy is reflected in his ongoing work in 'Marshals,' where he continues to portray characters that resonate with his cultural background. Through his performances, he aims to foster understanding and empathy towards the challenges faced by Indigenous communities.

### The Call for Justice

While the details surrounding Cole's death remain unclear, Mo Brings Plenty is vocal about seeking justice for his nephew. He has expressed his desire for accountability and transparency, urging authorities to thoroughly investigate the circumstances leading to Cole's passing. "We owe it to him and to our community to ensure that this does not go unanswered," he remarked, underscoring the broader implications of his family's tragedy.

In addition to his personal grief, Brings Plenty's advocacy extends to raising awareness about the systemic issues that affect Native American youth. He hopes that by sharing his story, he can inspire others to take action and support initiatives that promote justice and healing within Indigenous communities.

### Looking Ahead

As Mo Brings Plenty continues his journey in the entertainment industry, he remains steadfast in his mission to honor his nephew's legacy. He believes that by amplifying Indigenous voices and stories, he can contribute to a more inclusive and understanding society. "Cole was a bright light in our lives, and I want to make sure that his memory inspires change," he concluded.

Through his work in 'Marshals' and his commitment to advocacy, Mo Brings Plenty is not only navigating the complexities of grief but is also striving to create a lasting impact in the world around him. His dedication to justice and representation serves as a reminder of the power of storytelling and the importance of honoring those we have lost.
