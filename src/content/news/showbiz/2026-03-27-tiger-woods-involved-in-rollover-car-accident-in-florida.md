## Tiger Woods Involved in Rollover Car Accident in Florida

Golf legend Tiger Woods was involved in a rollover car accident on Friday afternoon in Jupiter Island, Florida. According to local authorities, the incident occurred shortly after 2 p.m. near 281 Beach Road, as reported by the Martin County Sheriff’s Office.

### Details of the Incident

The specifics surrounding the accident remain unclear at this time. Initial reports indicate that Woods' vehicle rolled over, but further information regarding the circumstances leading to the crash has not been disclosed. As of now, it is also uncertain whether Woods sustained any injuries from the incident.

### Response from Authorities

The Martin County Sheriff’s Office is currently investigating the accident. They have not released additional details regarding the crash or the condition of Woods. Local news outlets are closely monitoring the situation and are expected to provide updates as more information becomes available.
