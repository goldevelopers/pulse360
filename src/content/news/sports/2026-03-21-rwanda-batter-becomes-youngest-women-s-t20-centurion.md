## Rwanda's Fanny Utagushimaninde Becomes Youngest Women's T20 Centurion

In a remarkable achievement for Rwandan cricket, 15-year-old Fanny Utagushimaninde has made history by becoming the youngest woman to score a century in T20 international cricket. Utagushimaninde reached this milestone at the age of 15 years and 223 days, surpassing previous records and capturing the attention of cricket enthusiasts worldwide.
