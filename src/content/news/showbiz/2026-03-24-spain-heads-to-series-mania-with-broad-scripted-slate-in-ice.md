## Spain Showcases Scripted TV Ambitions at Series Mania

Spain is set to make a significant impact at the upcoming Series Mania Forum 2026, with a diverse slate of scripted television projects that reflect the country's growing confidence and ambition in the audiovisual sector. The initiative, presented by ICEX under the banner of Audiovisual from Spain, will be prominently featured in Lille on March 24, 2026.

### A Growing Presence in Global Television

The presentation, titled "Coming Next From Spain," aims to highlight the breadth of Spain's scripted television offerings. This event is part of a broader strategy to enhance the visibility of Spanish productions on the international stage, showcasing the creativity and innovation that the Spanish television industry has to offer.

Spain's participation in Series Mania is not merely a showcase of content; it is a testament to the country's commitment to expanding its footprint in the global entertainment landscape. The Spanish television industry has seen substantial growth in recent years, with a surge in high-quality productions that have garnered international acclaim. This includes a variety of genres, from drama to comedy, underscoring the versatility and depth of Spanish storytelling.

### ICEX's Role in Promoting Spanish Audiovisuals

ICEX, the Spanish Institute for Foreign Trade, plays a pivotal role in promoting Spanish audiovisual content abroad. By organizing events like "Coming Next From Spain," ICEX aims to facilitate connections between Spanish creators and international buyers, producers, and distributors. This initiative is part of a broader effort to position Spain as a key player in the global television market.

The event will feature a selection of upcoming series, providing attendees with a glimpse into the future of Spanish television. It is expected to attract industry professionals, including producers, broadcasters, and streaming platforms, all looking to explore potential collaborations and acquisitions.

### The Significance of Series Mania

Series Mania has established itself as one of the leading events in the television industry, drawing attention from around the globe. It serves as a platform for creators to present their work, network with industry peers, and engage in discussions about the future of television. Spain's participation reflects the country's recognition of the importance of such forums in fostering international partnerships and promoting cultural exchange.

As the Spanish television industry continues to evolve, events like Series Mania will play a crucial role in shaping its future. The emphasis on scripted content not only highlights Spain's rich storytelling tradition but also its ability to adapt to changing viewer preferences in a competitive global market.

### Conclusion

Spain's presence at Series Mania 2026, through the "Coming Next From Spain" initiative, underscores the nation's ambition to solidify its status as a leader in the global scripted television landscape. With ICEX's support and a diverse array of upcoming projects, Spain is poised to make a lasting impression at this prestigious event, further enhancing its reputation as a hub of creativity and innovation in the audiovisual sector.
