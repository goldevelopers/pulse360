## MHz Choice Acquires South African Drama ‘Cousins’ for U.S. Launch

In a significant development for global streaming content, MHz Choice has announced the acquisition of the acclaimed South African drama series "Cousins." The series, which is set to launch in North America in August, is based on a harrowing true crime case that has captured the attention of audiences both locally and internationally.

### Overview of ‘Cousins’

"Cousins" tells the gripping story of two twelve-year-old cousins, Issie Fourie and Petro Nel, who were kidnapped from their quiet hometown in South Africa's Free State province. The narrative delves into the emotional and psychological ramifications of the crime, exploring themes of family, loss, and resilience. The series is notable not only for its compelling storyline but also for its authentic representation of South African culture and language, being primarily filmed in Afrikaans.

### Significance of the Acquisition

The decision by MHz Choice to bring "Cousins" to the U.S. market underscores the growing interest in international programming among American audiences. As streaming platforms increasingly seek diverse content to attract subscribers, shows like "Cousins" serve to broaden the scope of available narratives, offering viewers a glimpse into different cultures and societal issues.

MHz Choice, known for its focus on international crime dramas and thrillers, aims to cater to an audience that appreciates nuanced storytelling and complex characters. The addition of "Cousins" to their lineup is expected to resonate with viewers who are drawn to true crime narratives, particularly those that highlight real-life events.

### Cultural Impact and Reception

Since its debut, "Cousins" has received critical acclaim in South Africa, praised for its writing, direction, and performances. The series has sparked conversations about safety, community, and the impact of crime on families, making it a poignant addition to the genre of crime drama. Its real-life inspiration adds a layer of depth that often resonates with viewers, making the story not just entertaining but also thought-provoking.

The show's reception in South Africa may serve as a barometer for its success in the U.S. market. As American audiences become more familiar with international content, the potential for "Cousins" to gain a following appears promising. The series not only entertains but also educates viewers about the societal issues faced in different parts of the world.

### Conclusion

The acquisition of "Cousins" by MHz Choice reflects a broader trend in the entertainment industry towards globalization and cultural exchange. As streaming services continue to expand their offerings, audiences are presented with a wealth of stories that transcend geographical boundaries. "Cousins," with its compelling narrative rooted in real events, is poised to make a significant impact in the North American market, further enriching the landscape of international television. 

With its anticipated launch in August, viewers can look forward to experiencing a unique blend of drama, culture, and the complexities of human relationships as portrayed in this South African gem.
