## Emery Backs Watkins Following England Omission

Aston Villa manager Unai Emery has expressed unwavering support for striker Ollie Watkins after the forward was notably excluded from the England national team squad announced by head coach Thomas Tuchel. This endorsement comes on the heels of Watkins' impressive performance in Villa's recent 2-0 victory over West Ham United, where he played a crucial role in securing the win.

### A Strong Performance

In the match against West Ham, Watkins demonstrated his goal-scoring prowess, contributing significantly to Aston Villa's success. His ability to find the back of the net has been a key factor in the team's strong form this season, making his omission from the England squad all the more surprising to many observers. Emery praised Watkins for his determination and resilience, stating, "He's a fighter," and emphasizing the importance of maintaining confidence despite the setback.

### The England Squad Selection

Thomas Tuchel's decision to leave Watkins out of the squad has sparked discussions among fans and analysts alike. The England team is preparing for upcoming international fixtures, and the selection process often draws scrutiny, especially when high-performing players are overlooked. Watkins, who has been a consistent performer in the Premier League, was expected by some to be included, given his recent form.

### Emery's Confidence in Watkins

Emery's comments reflect a broader sentiment within the Aston Villa camp regarding Watkins' capabilities. The manager highlighted that the striker's work ethic and commitment to the team are invaluable assets. "Ollie has shown that he can rise to the occasion," Emery noted, reinforcing the belief that his player will continue to contribute positively to the squad.

### Looking Ahead

As Aston Villa prepares for their next matches, the focus will remain on building momentum and securing vital points in the Premier League. Emery's faith in Watkins is likely to bolster the striker's confidence as he continues to strive for excellence on the pitch. The manager's backing may also serve as a reminder to the England coaching staff of Watkins' potential, particularly as the national team looks to solidify its attacking options.

### Conclusion

Ollie Watkins' exclusion from the England squad has raised eyebrows, but with strong support from his manager Unai Emery, the forward is poised to continue making significant contributions to Aston Villa. As the team looks to capitalize on their recent success, the focus will be on harnessing the talents of players like Watkins, whose fighting spirit and determination are essential for the challenges ahead.
