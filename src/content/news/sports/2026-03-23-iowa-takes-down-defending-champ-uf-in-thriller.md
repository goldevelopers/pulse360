## Iowa Upsets Defending Champion Florida in NCAA Tournament Thriller

In a stunning turn of events during the second round of the men's NCAA tournament, the Iowa Hawkeyes defeated the defending national champion Florida Gators, thanks to a clutch three-pointer by Alvaro Folgueiras with just 4.5 seconds left on the clock. The victory not only marks a significant upset but also propels Iowa into the next round of the tournament, leaving fans and analysts alike in awe of the Hawkeyes' performance.

Florida, known for its robust defense and high-scoring offense, initially took control of the game, establishing a lead in the first half. However, Iowa, displaying resilience and determination, fought back, narrowing the gap and eventually taking the lead in the closing minutes.

Throughout the game, the atmosphere was electric, with fans from both sides passionately supporting their teams. The tension reached its peak in the final moments, as Iowa and Florida exchanged baskets, setting the stage for a dramatic finish.

### Folgueiras' Heroic Moment

With the clock winding down and Iowa trailing by two points, Folgueiras received the ball beyond the arc. The sophomore guard, who has been a key player for the Hawkeyes this season, demonstrated remarkable poise under pressure.
