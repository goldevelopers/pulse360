## Corey Feldman Responds to Exclusion from Rob Reiner Oscars Tribute

During the 98th Academy Awards, a tribute was held honoring the esteemed director Rob Reiner and his late wife, Michele. The tribute featured many notable figures from Reiner's illustrious career, yet one prominent actor was noticeably absent: Corey Feldman, who starred in Reiner's acclaimed 1986 film "Stand by Me."

Feldman, a well-known figure in Hollywood, expressed his feelings about not being included in the tribute. In a brief statement, he remarked, “It is what it is,” indicating a sense of acceptance regarding his exclusion from the event. His response reflects a calm demeanor, suggesting that he holds no ill will towards the organizers of the tribute or the event itself.

Rob Reiner, who has directed several iconic films, including "When Harry Met Sally..." and "The Princess Bride," has had a significant impact on the film industry. "Stand by Me," based on a novella by Stephen King, is often regarded as a classic coming-of-age story and has left a lasting legacy. Feldman played one of the four young boys on a journey to find a missing body, a role that contributed to his rise as a prominent actor during the 1980s.

The absence of Feldman from the tribute has sparked discussions among fans and industry observers. Some have expressed surprise, given his connection to one of Reiner's most beloved films. However, tributes at high-profile events like the Oscars often involve complex decisions regarding whom to include, and not every collaborator can be represented.

Feldman's career has seen its share of ups and downs, and he has been vocal about various issues within the entertainment industry. His reaction to the Oscars tribute exclusion may reflect his broader perspective on the industry and his experiences within it.

As the film community continues to celebrate Reiner's contributions, the focus remains on the legacy he has created through his work. The tribute served as a reminder of the collaborative nature of filmmaking and the many individuals who have played a role in bringing stories to life on the big screen.

While Feldman's absence may have been felt by some, it is clear that the celebration of Rob Reiner's work will continue to resonate with audiences and inspire future generations of filmmakers and actors alike.
